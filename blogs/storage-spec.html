  • http://e.huawei.com/en/products/cloud-computing-dc/storage/unified-storage/6800f-v5
Model OceanStor 6800 V5
Architecture SmartMatrix 2.0 all-flash architecture
Latency < 1 ms
Maximum Number of Controllers 8
Processors Multi-core processors
System Cache (expands with the number of controllers) 512 GB to 8 TB
Supported Storage Protocols Fibre Channel, FCoE, iSCSI, InfiniBand, NFS, CIFS, FTP, and HTTP
Types of Front-end Ports 16 Gbit/s Fibre Channel, 8 Gbit/s Fibre Channel, 10 Gbit/s FCoE, 1/10 Gbit/s Ethernet, and 56 Gbit/s InfiniBand

Virtualization Environments Virtualization platforms: FusionSphere, VMware, XenServer, and Hyper-V; Value-added features: VMware VAAI/VASA/SRM, Hyper-V; vSphere, vCenter
Data Protection Software HyperSnap (snapshot), HyperClone (clone); HyperCopy (copy), HyperMirror (volume mirroring); HyperMetro (active-active arrays), HyperReplication (remote replication); HyperLock (WORM), HyperVault (all-in-one backup)
Mission-critical Business Protection SmartQoS (intelligent service quality control); SmartPartition (intelligent partitioning);
