1 IEEE - International Conference on Networking, Architecture, and Storage

  • Search the paper in IEEE: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org
    • example: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/5283088/
  • Insert sci-hub.st after the domain name.
    • example: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org.sci-hub.st/document/5283088/

2 FAST - File and Storage Technologies

  • 2020: https://www.usenix.org/conference/fast20/technical-sessions
    • 2020-full: https://www.usenix.org/sites/default/files/fast20_full-proceedings.pdf
    • 2020-contents: https://www.usenix.org/sites/default/files/fast20_contents.pdf
  • 2021: https://www.usenix.org/conference/fast21/technical-sessions
    • 2021-full: https://www.usenix.org/sites/default/files/fast21_full-proceedings.pdf
  • Register
    • https://www.usenix.org/user/password

3 ACMDL - Association for Computing Machinery Digital Library

  • 在ACM网站找到想要的论文，比如ARKDB：https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3448016.3457553
  • 把doi/后面的部分拷贝下来，如10.1145/3448016.3457553
  • 打开文献小镇: http://www.sci-hub.ac.cn/ , 把上面拷贝内容粘贴进去，打开即可下载；
